About Me

Hello thanks for stopping by to take a look at my life. I was born in Blackfoot Idaho February 9th 1962 (don't remember a thing about it). Our family moved to Minnesota in 1964 and I've been here ever since. I am married with two kids boy and a girl. I have owned many business and love to start new one. I like to grow stuff and make music. I have a band called Dakota Clover which I'm sure you have heard of and if not you need to.
